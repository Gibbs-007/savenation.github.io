About Us

Save Nation is the student community initiative volunteering movement to fight aginst corona impacts. We tried to solve imacts of corona using technology. We are group of 100+ volunteers from various part of india primarly solve migration problems and other major imacts of covid-19. We are looking more volunteers from various domains like technology, social, call centre. We already run the help line of 25+ volunteers to address the untapped problems of government.

At this stage our goverment expecting support from us. We already step forward. Are you ? We welcoming Universities, Colleges, Student Communities, all state governments, central government departments, NGO's, Tech Starups to save many lifes.
